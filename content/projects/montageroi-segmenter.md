## Project Overview

SympathiQ is a holistic wellness platform that connects users with certified coaches across multiple wellness domains including mental health, fitness, and nutrition. The platform provides interactive coaching sessions, habit tracking tools, and comprehensive wellness resources designed to support personal growth, resilience, and overall well-being.

## Project Details

### Technologies Used

* Video Conferencing
* Habit Tracking Systems
* Wellness Analytics
* Coach Matching Algorithms
* Resource Management

### Key Features

* **Certified Coaches**: Access to qualified coaches in mental health, fitness, and nutrition
* **Interactive Sessions**: Live and recorded coaching sessions
* **Habit Tracking**: Tools to monitor and improve wellness habits
* **Wellness Resources**: Comprehensive library of wellness content and resources
* **Personal Growth Support**: Structured programs for personal development
* **Resilience Building**: Tools and techniques to build mental and physical resilience

## Project Overview

Kitt (KitaHQ) is an AI-powered HR software platform that automates and streamlines the recruitment process. The platform uses artificial intelligence to automate resume screening, conduct AI video interviews, and perform candidate assessments, enabling teams to hire faster, reduce bias, and evaluate applicants at scale.

## Project Details

### Technologies Used

* Artificial Intelligence
* Natural Language Processing
* Video Interview Technology
* Resume Parsing
* Assessment Automation

### Key Features

* **AI Resume Screening**: Automated resume analysis and candidate ranking
* **AI Video Interviews**: Intelligent video interview system with automated evaluation
* **Candidate Assessments**: Automated skills and competency assessments
* **Bias Reduction**: AI-driven processes designed to reduce hiring bias
* **Scalable Evaluation**: Process large numbers of applicants efficiently
* **Faster Hiring**: Streamlined recruitment workflow for quicker hiring decisions

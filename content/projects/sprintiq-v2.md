## Project Overview

SprintiQ v2 is an AI-native agile planning and sprint management platform designed for development teams. It combines traditional sprint and backlog management with AI-driven assistance to streamline planning, prioritization, and delivery.

## Project Details

### Technologies Used

* AI / LLM integration
* Agile & sprint management
* Modern web stack (Vercel)
* SaaS product experience

### Key Features

* **AI-native planning**: Agile and sprint planning enhanced with AI assistance
* **Sprint management**: Plan, track, and manage sprints in one place
* **Backlog & workflows**: Organize work and run agile workflows effectively

## Portfolio Highlights

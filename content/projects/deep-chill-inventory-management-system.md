## Project Overview

Mobirest is a mobile-first business services portal designed to handle bookings, customer interactions, and mobile commerce operations. The platform emphasizes responsive design, exceptional mobile user experience, and comprehensive business operations tooling, making it ideal for businesses that prioritize mobile engagement.

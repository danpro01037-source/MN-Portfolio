## Project Overview

DreamKid AI is an AI-powered storytelling platform that generates personalized illustrated stories for children, with a special focus on those in hospitals. The platform creates unique adventures tailored to each child's name, age, and personal situation, designed to encourage imagination, provide comfort, and build resilience during challenging times.

## Project Details

### Technologies Used

* Artificial Intelligence
* Natural Language Processing
* Story Generation
* Personalization Algorithms
* Illustration Systems

### Key Features

* **Personalized Stories**: Custom stories tailored to each child's name, age, and situation
* **Hospital-Focused**: Specialized content for children in medical settings
* **Illustrated Adventures**: Rich visual storytelling with custom illustrations
* **Emotional Design**: Carefully crafted to provide comfort and encouragement
* **Resilience Building**: Stories designed to help children cope with challenges

## Project Overview

TrackHero is an experience management system specifically designed for automotive clubs and race venues. The platform centralizes customer management, event coordination, bookings, payments, and fleet logistics, providing a comprehensive solution for managing all aspects of automotive club and race venue operations.

## Project Details

### Technologies Used

* Event Management Systems
* Fleet Management Tools
* Booking & Reservation Systems
* Payment Processing
* Customer Relationship Management

### Key Features

* **Customer Management**: Comprehensive customer database and relationship management
* **Event Management**: Full event lifecycle management from planning to execution
* **Booking System**: Advanced booking and reservation management
* **Payment Processing**: Integrated payment handling for events and services
* **Fleet Logistics**: Management of vehicle fleets and logistics
* **Operational Dashboards**: Centralized dashboards for operations management

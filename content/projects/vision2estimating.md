## Project Overview

Vision 2 Estimating (V2E) is a construction estimating and building estimator platform designed for custom home builders and construction companies. Using 3D estimating technology, V2E helps eliminate guesswork, reduce costly errors, and deliver precise material takeoffs, labour requirements, and professional quotes. The platform serves builders across Australia, NZ, and beyond with a focus on accuracy, workflow integration, and client-ready proposals.

## Project Details

### Technologies Used

* 3D modeling and takeoff technology
* Construction estimating workflows
* Integration with job management systems (e.g. Wunderbuild)
* Quiz and lead-generation flows
* Responsive marketing and product site

### Key Features

* **3D estimating**: Visual, real-time takeoffs so nothing is missed; accurate material quantities and labour requirements
* **Error reduction**: Catch mistakes before they become costly change orders or rework
* **Quote refinement & client presentation**: Turn estimates into clear, professional proposals with 3D models that build trust
* **Workflow integration**: Estimates flow into existing project management tools to save time and reduce double data entry
* **Quiz & demos**: Interactive quiz and free demo booking to engage prospects and qualify leads
